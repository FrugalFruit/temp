ABOUT US

Artberry is a team of professionals. We started in 2014. Today we have 150 hectares in Ukraine filled with orchards and berry fields. Each berry varieties we grow are selected for their premium flavor, size and harvest timing to create a seamless supply of top quality berries from Ukraine. We have Global G.A.P, GRASP, SMETA audit certification and are a SEDEX member.

BLUEBERRY

Our main product is blueberries, and we are glad to offer you such famous varieties of blueberries as Duke, Draper, Liberty and Aurora. The average size of one berry is 1,4 - 1,9 cm, the average weight is 1,5 - 1,7 g. These varieties provide fruiting from June to October. Storage in special conditions allows to extend the term of fresh berries until December.

Planting Material from Fall Creek

All planting material is purchased directly from Fall Creek, a US-based nursery with a keen focus on blueberry genetics, plants and grower support delivered to the world's premier blueberry growers.
  • High quality plants, perfectly adapted to climate conditions at grower's location

  • High fruit yield

  • Exceptional fruit quality and taste

  • Technical support at every stage

  • Annual online and offline training

  • Continuous breeding program with new varieties for future farm expansion

Growing process:

  • Open ground

  • Availability from June to September

  • Advanced technical approach

  • Absence of GMOs, additives and preservatives, chemical treatment after harvest.
